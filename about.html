The Path We Must Take

With the current World population growth rate and the enlargement of emerging countries' middle classes, consensus forecasts predict a relevant increase of average meat consumption in the next decades.
The related consequences in terms of land, water and animal exploitation will be unbearable for our planet: technology and progress in the food industry are necessary today!

MeatSeed supports three main product innovations that are likely to lead the transition to a sustainable and circular meat production.

Three main innovations

Plant-Based
Meat

Plant-based meat is obtained combining different vegetables: the variety of mixes allows the production of flavors and textures that resemble real meat with increasing success over time. The benefits are fot the environment - less water, greenhouses and land exploitation - and for our health, since plant-foods are free of cholesterol and scientifically proven to be healthier for humans.

Cultured
Meat

Cultured Meat is the result of in vitro agriculture of animal cells. It is a great alternative for the most affectionate consumers of animal meat, since the texture is the same and the taste almost identical. It is the most recent, capital intensive and economically challenging technology of the three considered, however cultured meat is expected to become cost-competitive to traditional meat in matter of years.

Insect-Based
Meat

Easy and convenient to grow, industrially scalable and full of nutrients. There is only one issue with the adoption of insect as human food: they are insects! Cultural barriers to insect-based meat are high, especially in EU and USA. Therefore, many companies are focusing on pet and livestock feeding as a reasonable midpoint of a larger adoption.
