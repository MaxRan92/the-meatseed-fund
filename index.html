The MeatSeed Fund

The MeatSeed Fund is a global venture fund that invests in startups focusing on innovative technology for meat alternatives production. Since 2010, The MeatSeed Fund has been an active investor in the food industry and has a portfolio of over 8 companies worldwide. The main purpose is to back entrepreneurs with the support needed to share their ideas, get feedback from experts and potential investors and receive coaching for business development.
