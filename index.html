The MeatSeed Fund

The MeatSeed Fund is a global venture fund that invests in startups with innovative meat alternatives. Since 2010, The MeatSeed Fund has been active in the food industry and has a portfolio of over 20 companies worldwide. We focus on early stage investments and offer entrepreneurs an opportunity to share their ideas, get feedback from experts and potential investors, and receive coaching for business development.
